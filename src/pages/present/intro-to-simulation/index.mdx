## The Exponential Distribution

---

The exponential distribution models the time between events. If events arrive at rate $\lambda$ per unit time, the waiting time until the next event follows:

$$f(x) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x}, \quad x \ge 0$$

The mean waiting time is $\frac{1}{\lambda}$.
